This is an application that helps an individual to know which day of the week he/she was born,by only entering the date,month and year that he/she was born in .In addition it also show an akan name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture,where children given names according to the day of week they were born.

Absolutly in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds day of week they had born on. this akan names differs according the rgion someone born in and the lamguage they use such as fante ,twi and so many others.
